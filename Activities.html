Conferences

Chapter Officer Retreat for Excellence (CORE)
CORE provides Georgia TSA local chapter officers with an opportunity to develop interpersonal and leadership skills, gain necessary knowledge and abilities to carry out the duties of their individual offices, and prepare a Chapter Program of Work for the upcoming year.


Technology Day
Technology Day at the Georgia National Fair provides Georgia TSA members with an opportunity to compete in various competitions to win money for their chapter and participate in a motivational rally to help get you pumped up and excited about the year to come. Several of our members placed in the top ten in their events.


Fall Leadership Conference (FLC)
FLC provides Georgia TSA members and advisors the opportunity to increase their leadership, communication, and team building skills by attending hands-on workshops, seminars and general sessions. This conference also provides for chapter members and advisors to gain knowledge and skills in relation to our competitive events by attending hands on workshops, seminars, and sessions during our Technical Sessions. Our chapter was awarded the Superior Chapter Champion Award based on our performance at Technology Day. Our VEX team placed second and our electric car team place fifth.


State Leadership Conference (SLC)
Georgia TSA's annual program of leadership development and competitive events provide students opportunities beyond the traditional classroom/laboratory setting which add to their increased knowledge and understanding of an ever-changing technical world. Our chapters has placed in the top ten in many events and have placed third in the state consecutively for 3 years.


National Leadership Conference (NLC)
The TSA national conference is packed with competitive events and challenging activities that foster personal growth and leadership development. Each year TSA members are offered these and many more opportunities at the national conference. Our chapter has placed in the top ten for several events.


Leukemia and Lymphoma Light the Night Walk - Our chapter participates in a walk to help find a cure and spread awareness for Leukemia and Lymphoma.

VEX Robotics Competition - Our chapter hosts an annual VEX competition for middle and high schoolers.
